"The greatest gift you can give someone is to tell them who they are"

Let me help you find your family

You know how some people will do ANYTHING for money? Or a taco....or whatever? Well, replace money or a taco with DNA / genealogical research. That is me! And I am NOT kidding. I was lucky enough to be surrounded with SEVERAL adoptees in my life who wanted to track down their biological relatives AND once I started "the hunt" I was hooked. In 2012 I had my DNA done (sorry Ancestry, the kit I was given was from 23andme.) and then I FORCED my parents (WHO I ALREADY KNEW I WAS RELATED TO) to complete their DNA. After that, I studied all kinds of ways to help people. I joined NAMus and looked into cold cases near me. I assisted friends, neighbors, and even complete strangers online in understanding their DNA. I TRULY enjoy the monotonous hours involved in getting the answers. I absolutely believe I could be an asset to your company even if it isn't with this specific position. I hope you consider my resume because I say the words "ancestry" or "DNA" every single day in one form or another and quite frankly, the people around me are quite tired of hearing about it.
